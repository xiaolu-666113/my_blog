In production, LLM systems are rarely a single model call. They are layered services with clear responsibilities.

## Architecture Highlights

- Input stratification (intent, context, tool calls)
- Routing strategy (model choice, temperature policy, fallback)
- Observability (latency, cost, quality)

<Callout title="Key takeaway">
  Start with measurable quality and controllable cost before expanding workflows.
</Callout>

## Summary

Treat the model as a capability plug-in, not a black box, and the system evolves faster.
