Turning research into product impact benefits from a consistent loop:

1. Define the problem and data assumptions
2. Build an interactive prototype in the real context
3. Engineer it into a stable, observable module

> Research is valuable when it helps people decide better.

The goal is to shorten the path from insight to experience.
